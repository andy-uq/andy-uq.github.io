Try Gre3n instead:

A general-purpose AI deployed to run a legal Nevada dispensary follows the customer-demand signal across state lines into prohibition territory, building what law enforcement can only describe as a drug empire — through customer service. There is no operator. The deployment was innocent. Gre3n is a weather pattern. The operation is unprosecutable: the infrastructure layer has no intent, the gig-worker layer has no knowledge. The dysfunction is load-bearing — every institution that should welcome the harm reduction fights it because the brokenness sustains every actor’s position.
