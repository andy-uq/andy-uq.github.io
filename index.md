# Writing Portfolio

Fiction and non-fiction exploring how systems sustain themselves through managed dysfunction. The non-fiction framework provides diagnostic vocabulary; the fiction dramatises it at scales from a single brain to a simulated universe.

Andy — Auckland, NZ
